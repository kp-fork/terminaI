## Criteria Used

1. ✅ OSS with permissive license (MIT, Apache 2.0, AGPL)
2. ✅ AI/agent extensible (voice, NL, automation)
3. ✅ Large addressable market (millions of users)
4. ✅ "Boring" but functional baseline
5. ✅ No dominant commercial fork
